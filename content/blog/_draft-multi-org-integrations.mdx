## Outline (work in progress)

I'm currently working on a deep-dive about multi-org Salesforce architectures. Here's my rough outline — feedback welcome.

### 1. When you actually need multiple orgs
- M&A scenarios
- Regional compliance (data residency)
- Sandboxing for sensitive divisions

### 2. Integration approaches compared
- **Salesforce-to-Salesforce (S2S)** — built-in, but limited
- **Platform Events** — async, scalable, my preferred default
- **Middleware** (MuleSoft, Boomi) — when you need transformation/orchestration
- **Custom REST APIs** — when you need fine control

### 3. Decision framework
A flowchart for choosing the right approach based on:
- Data volume
- Latency requirements
- Transformation complexity
- Budget

### 4. Real-world example
The architecture I designed for a client connecting their EU and US orgs while staying GDPR-compliant.
