## Hello, world! 👋

After 7+ years of building on the Salesforce platform, I've decided to start writing publicly about what I've learned. This first post is a little about who I am and what you can expect from this blog.

## Who I am

I'm a Senior Salesforce Engineer based in Bengaluru, India. I've spent the last several years building solutions across:

- **Sales Cloud & Service Cloud** — automation, process design, custom UIs
- **Apex & LWC** — clean architecture, performance optimization
- **Integrations** — REST APIs, Platform Events, async patterns

## Why I'm writing

Three reasons:

1. **Teaching is the best way to learn.** Forcing myself to explain a concept clearly is the fastest path to understanding it deeply.
2. **The Salesforce ecosystem moves fast.** I want a record of how I'm thinking *right now* — what's working, what's not, what I'd do differently.
3. **Community.** I've learned almost everything I know from blog posts, talks, and Trailhead. This is my way of giving back.

## What to expect

I'll write about:

- **Architecture deep-dives** — how to think about real-world Salesforce problems
- **Code patterns** — Apex frameworks, LWC patterns, integration design
- **Lessons from production** — what went wrong, what I'd change

Posts will be honest, opinionated, and grounded in what I've actually shipped. No fluff, no listicles — just things I think are worth your time.
